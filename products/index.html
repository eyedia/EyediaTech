Photo platform

Eyedeea Photos

AI-powered photo intelligence and curation for large, messy libraries.

Eyedeea Photos helps people understand what they have, reduce clutter, preserve folder control, and rediscover meaningful moments across web, mobile, desktop, and TV-friendly experiences.

  • Applies AI-driven photo understanding to auto-curate raw libraries into structured event albums.
  • Ingests photos from local storage, NAS and Synology sources, and cloud-connected libraries.
  • Shows collection intelligence through KYP with totals, curated counts, duplicates, blurry images, and storage usage.
